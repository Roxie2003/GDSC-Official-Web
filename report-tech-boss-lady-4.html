Tech Boss Lady #4

Live (Full Video):


Summary of event:

The event featuring Esha Singh, a Data Analytics Associate at ZS Associates and former Software Development Intern at Amazon, provided invaluable insights into navigating the journey from academia to professional success. Her narrative emphasised resilience in the face of rejection, turning setbacks into opportunities for growth. She stressed the importance of starting early in both technical and non-technical skill development, offering guidance on when and how to begin coding and preparing for various career paths. Additionally, She provided clarity on how to identify one's passions and choose the most suitable career field, highlighting the significance of self-reflection and exploration
Throughout the event, She shared a wealth of resources and practical advice for aspiring professionals. She detailed the application processes for internships and job placements, directing attendees to platforms where they could discover relevant opportunities. Furthermore, She provided strategies for leveraging professional connections, offering examples of how to approach individuals for referrals. Her insights extended to crafting standout CVs and excelling in interviews, with emphasis on key tactics to impress potential employers and secure dream positions. The event concluded in a dynamic question and answer session, allowing participants to glean further insights and personalised advice from Esha Singh based on their specific career aspirations and challenges. Overall, the event served as a comprehensive guide for navigating the competitive landscape of the tech industry and beyond.


Benefits:

• Enhanced resilience and rejection management skills.
• Early preparation for technical and non-technical career paths.
• Practical insights into internship and job application processes.
• Improved networking and professional development strategies.
